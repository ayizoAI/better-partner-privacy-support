# better-partner-privacy-support

Privacy Policy
Last updated: January 2026
Introduction
Better Partner ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our relationship wellness application.
Information We Collect
We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

    - Account information (email address)
    - Profile information (partner name, relationship details)
    - Memories and notes you save
    - Date planning and reflection data
    - Onboarding preferences

How We Use Your Information
Your data is used solely to provide and improve the app experience:

    - Personalizing insights and date suggestions
    - Storing your memories and date history
    - Syncing data across your devices
    - Processing subscription payments

Data Storage and Security
Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use Supabase for database management with row-level security policies, ensuring only you can access your data. We do not sell, share, or provide your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.
Data Retention
We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting our support team.
Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:

    - Supabase for authentication and database
    - RevenueCat for subscription management
    - Apple App Store and Google Play for payment processing

Your Rights
You have the right to:

    - Access your personal data
    - Correct inaccurate data
    - Request deletion of your data
    - Export your data

Contact Us
If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at creators@ayizo.com
